# Tomato Ketchup
Tomato ketchup is a vaguely tomato-based condiment with a sweet and sour flavour.

## Ingredients
* 1 tin chopped tomatoes
* 1/2 cup white sugar
* 1/2 cup white vinegar
* 1/2 tsp onion powder
* 1/2 tsp garlic powder
* 1 tsp salt

## Instructions
* blend chopped tomatoes
* mix in other ingredients
* simmer for 2h
* let cool to room temperature
